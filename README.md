# Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

**Project Title**: Marketing Campaign Performance Dashboard  
**Tool Used**: Power BI Desktop  
**Purpose**: To analyze and visualize multi-channel marketing campaign performance to drive data-driven marketing decisions.

This interactive dashboard provides a comprehensive view of campaign effectiveness across different channels, regions, audience segments, and time periods by tracking key metrics such as impressions, clicks, conversions, spend, revenue, and ROI.

## Executive Summary

- **Dataset**: 820 marketing campaigns with details on channel, region, audience, and performance metrics.
- **Methods Used**: Data modeling, DAX measures, interactive visualizations, and slicer synchronization.
- **Goal**: Help marketing teams identify high-performing campaigns, optimize budget allocation, and improve ROI.

## Key Highlights

- Interactive multi-page dashboard with clean navigation
- Channel-wise performance comparison (Spend vs Revenue vs ROI)
- Audience segmentation analysis (Age, Gender, New vs Returning)
- Time-series trends and campaign-level deep dive
- Fully filterable with synchronized slicers across pages

## Business Problem

Marketing teams lacked a centralized, visual platform to:
- Compare performance across channels and campaigns
- Track ROI and conversion efficiency
- Identify underperforming segments quickly

## Methodology

- **Data Preparation**: Cleaned and transformed raw campaign data, created Date table for time intelligence
- **Data Modeling**: Established relationships between campaign and date tables
- **DAX Measures**: Created calculations for Profit, ROI %, CTR, Conversion Rate, Campaign Duration
- **Visualization**: Built 4-page interactive dashboard with cards, bar charts, line charts, donut charts, and tables

## Skills Demonstrated

- Power BI Dashboard Development
- DAX Calculations & Time Intelligence
- Data Modeling & Relationships
- Interactive Design & User Experience
- Analytical Storytelling

## Results & Insights

- Identified top-performing channels and campaigns by ROI
- Revealed significant differences in performance between New and Returning audiences
- Highlighted seasonal and regional trends in conversions
- Provided clear recommendations for budget reallocation
